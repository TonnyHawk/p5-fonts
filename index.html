The >>Yom-Tool<< is an interactive, generative type tool designed to visualize your health data in a truly unique way. By analyzing your personal health metrics, the tool creates a personalized typographic representation of your health status using your initials.This innovative experience is part of YOM, the cutting-edge food vending system that crafts customized 3D-printed lunches tailored to your needs. Your personalized initials, reflecting your health data, are printed in real time on your lunch packaging, making your meal not only functional but also uniquely yours. Experience how your health comes to life with the <<Yom-Tool>>
